This Privacy Policy describes how Flag! ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application Flag! (the "App").

By downloading or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Stored Locally

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Game progress and statistics (high scores, rounds completed, etc.)
  • User preferences (sound settings)
  • Gameplay upgrades earned (boots level, gloves level)

This information is stored using Apple's standard UserDefaults system and remains on your device. We do not have access to this data.

1.2 Information Collected by Third Parties

The App displays advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks. These networks may collect certain information as described in their respective privacy policies, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region)
  • App usage data

Please refer to the privacy policies of our advertising partners for more information about their data practices.

2. How We Use Information

The locally stored information is used solely to:

  • Save your game progress
  • Remember your preferences
  • Track your personal statistics and achievements
  • Provide a personalized gaming experience

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties.

Third-party advertising networks operating within the App may collect and use data according to their own privacy policies for the purpose of serving relevant advertisements.

4. Data Retention

Game data is stored locally on your device until you:

  • Delete the App
  • Use the "Reset Progress" feature within the App
  • Clear the App's data through your device settings

5. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App does not require account creation or submission of any personal information to play.

6. Your Choices

You have the following options regarding your data:

  • Reset Progress: Use the in-app Settings menu to reset all game progress and statistics
  • Delete App: Removing the App from your device will delete all locally stored data
  • Limit Ad Tracking: Use your device's privacy settings to limit ad tracking (Settings > Privacy > Tracking on iOS)

7. Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms. No personal data is transmitted to our servers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on our website. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the email address on our Support page.

10. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since we do not collect personal information that is transmitted off your device, these rights are automatically preserved.

11. International Users

The App is operated from the United States. If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, please be aware that information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our advertising partners operate.
